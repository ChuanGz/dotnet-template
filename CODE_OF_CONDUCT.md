# Code of Conduct

This repository requires respectful, professional collaboration.

Challenge technical ideas with specific, evidence-based reasoning. Keep feedback focused on correctness, clarity, risk, trade-offs, and repository outcomes. The purpose of disagreement is to improve the repository, not to win an argument.

Personal criticism and attacks are not accepted. Do not target individuals, teams, companies, communities, technologies, or engineering styles. Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, abusive language, spam, and deliberately disruptive behavior are also prohibited.

Maintainers may edit, reject, remove, or restrict contributions and participation that violate these expectations.
